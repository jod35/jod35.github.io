NileJS is an emerging backend framework that aims to simplify and modernize how we build server-side applications in Typescript. In this conversation, I sat down with [Hussein Kizz](https://github.com/hussseinkizz), the creator of NileJS, to discuss the inspiration behind the framework, its core philosophy, and where it fits in the current backend development landscape.

## What is NileJS?

NileJS is designed from the ground up to provide a fresh, developer-friendly approach to backend web development. Unlike some of the more established frameworks, NileJS focuses on simplicity, performance, and developer experience.

## Key Discussion Points

- **The Origin Story**: Hussein shares what motivated him to create NileJS and the problems he aimed to solve
- **Core Philosophy**: Understanding the design principles that make NileJS unique
- **Use Cases**: When and why you should consider NileJS for your next project
- **The Future**: What's on the roadmap for NileJS
